Guitars Under the Stars

Guitars Under the Stars is a unique event that combine Astronomy and Classical Guitar and is hosted each year by the Department of Physics and Astronomy of Drake University at the Drake Municipal Observatory. The event stars with a classical guitar concert and then a talk is given by a Profesor in Astronomy to show the audience how to gaze stars with the telescopes. This picture was taken in 2016 when I played "The Cathedral" by Agustin Barrios which is one of my favorites classical guitar composers.
